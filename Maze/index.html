Introduction

Welcome to the virtual reality (VR) maze environment! You, as a participant, will engage in a total of three (3) different tasks during this ~30-minute session: a familiarisation task, a navigation task, and an estimation task.

YOU ARE WELCOME TO DISCONTINUE THE STUDY AT ANY TIME, WITH NO REPERCUSSIONS.

Instructions

The tasks will not begin until you complete the setup — a simple process of entering your participant ID into a text field — which you can do by pressing the ESC key.

After completing the setup, you will be prompted to press SPACE to start the familiarisation task.

Familiarisation Task

The familiarisation task is your opportunity to become familiar with the structure of the maze and with navigating through the environment using your mouse and keyboard. Controls are displayed at the bottom right of the program window.

The arrow keys on your keyboard will enable you to maneuver and explore the VR environment, and clicking and dragging with your mouse will rotate your view.

Although this task will end automatically after 5 minutes, you can move onto the next task earlier than that by pressing ENTER or RETURN.

After the task has finished, you will be prompted to press SPACE to begin the navigation task.

Navigation Task

There are eight (8) navigation trials.

In each one, you will start at one of four (4) predetermined starting locations, and will be tasked with locating an object (which will be indicated on the program window) in the maze as quickly as you can.

The target object will be a guitar, a cactus, a cabinet, a lamp post, a cat statue, or a clock.

Upon moving close to the target object, all objects in the maze and the maze itself will disappear, and you will be transported to the starting location of the next navigation trial. This will continue until all the navigation trials are complete.

Upon completion of the navigation task, you will be prompted to press SPACE to start the estimation task.

Estimation Task

There are twelve (12) estimation trials.

Similar to the navigation task, in each estimation trial, you will start at one of four (4) predetermined starting locations, and will be tasked with locating an object (which will be indicated on the program window) in the maze as quickly as you can.

The target object will be a guitar, a cactus, a cabinet, a lamp post, a cat statue, or a clock.

However, this time, upon moving close to the target object, all objects in the maze and the maze itself will disappear, and you will have to navigate to where you think you started the trial. Once you are confident in your location, press ENTER or RETURN to end the trial.This will continue until all the navigation trials are complete.

Upon completion of the estimation task, the program will end.
