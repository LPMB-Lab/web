BioMotion

Welcome to the BioMotion study.

Once the study has started, you will see a series of humanoid point light displays (PLDs) begin walking towards you and vanish after a few seconds.

When each PLD vanishes, indicate which direction (relative to you) you think the PLD was going to turn in before it vanished, using the ←, →, ↑, and ↓ keys.

For example, if you think the PLD was turning to your right just before it vanished, press the → key once it's gone. If you think it was heading right towards you, press either the ↑ or ↓ keys.

The controls are also shown on the study display for quick reference.

To begin the study, press SPACE.
