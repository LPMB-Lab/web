BioMotion
Press Space to start the BioMotion program. A PLD (point light display) will appear and begin walking towards you, and disappear after a short time. Just before it disappears, it will do one of three things:
  • continue going straight,
  • Start turning left, or
  • start turning right.
If you think the PLD continued go straight, press Up or Down; if you think it turned to your left, press Left; if you think it turned to your right, press Right.
